Refund Policy

How registration fees and refunds are handled on New Camp.

Last updated: July 2026

1. Registration fees

The one-time registration fee (Basic Listing ₹9,999, Angel Round ₹14,999, Venture Round ₹29,999, or Growth Round ₹39,999) covers listing on the platform and your initial consultation with our deal team.

2. Before the Service Agreement is signed

If you cancel before the Service Agreement is executed and before any consultation or preparatory work has begun, you may request a full refund within 7 days of payment by writing to desk@newcampcapital.com.

3. After onboarding begins

Once the Service Agreement is signed and onboarding, consultation or preparatory work (pitch review, financial structuring, KYC, etc.) has commenced, the registration fee is non-refundable, as it covers time and resources already committed by our deal team.

4. Success commission

Success commission is payable only when a transaction actually closes through the platform. If no transaction closes, no success commission is charged. Commission already paid on a closed transaction is non-refundable.

5. Platform-side cancellation

If New Camp is unable to onboard your mandate for reasons within our control (for example, failing internal compliance checks after payment), a full refund of the registration fee will be issued.

6. How to request a refund

Email desk@newcampcapital.com with your registered email and payment details. Eligible refunds are processed within 10–15 business days to the original payment method.

7. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us at desk@newcampcapital.com or call +91 99999 99999.
